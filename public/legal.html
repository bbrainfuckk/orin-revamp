Privacy policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

ORIN AI is a product of IDRA, Intelligence Design & Revenue Automation, led by Marvin Sarreal Villanueva in the Philippines. This policy covers orin.work, ORIN AI workspaces, and ORIN AI channel connections.

Information we handle

  • Account information supplied by the sign-in provider, such as name, email address, profile image, and account identifier.
  • Workspace settings, assistant instructions, approved knowledge, team access, automations, contacts, conversations, tasks, and analytics created by authorized workspace members.
  • Messages and account identifiers received from channels a business expressly connects, including supported Meta, commerce, social, website, and automation services.
  • Security and service data such as IP address, device and browser information, timestamps, request identifiers, delivery results, and usage measurements.
  • Provider credentials or authorization tokens supplied through an approved connection flow. These are encrypted and are not exposed to browser clients after storage.

How information is used

Information is used to authenticate users, deliver requested messages and automations, answer inquiries, route conversations, provide human handoff, measure service performance, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations. Approved business information and recent conversation context may be sent to the AI provider selected by the workspace to prepare a response.

Sharing and retention

IDRA does not sell personal data. Information may be processed by infrastructure, authentication, database, AI inference, messaging, commerce, automation, security, and support providers only as needed to operate the service. Connected providers also process data under their own terms. Data is retained only while needed for the workspace, security, dispute resolution, or legal obligations.

Business responsibility and human control

The business operating an ORIN AI workspace controls its connected accounts, approved knowledge, retention choices, and customer notices. It remains responsible for lawful instructions, consent, channel policies, and decisions that require professional or human judgment. ORIN AI supports escalation and does not replace emergency, medical, legal, financial, or public-safety professionals.

Your rights

Depending on applicable law, including the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012, a person may request access, correction, objection, erasure, blocking, or a copy of personal data. Submit a request through the IDRA contact page. We may verify identity and workspace authority before acting.

Data deletion instructions

For website, workspace, Meta, WhatsApp, and connected-channel data

  1. Open the IDRA contact page.
  2. State that the request is for “ORIN AI data deletion.”
  3. Provide the email used to sign in, the workspace or business name, and the connected channel or account involved. Do not send passwords, private keys, or access tokens.
  4. IDRA will verify the requester’s identity and authority. When verified, we will delete or de-identify the applicable account, workspace, contact, conversation, credential, and integration records unless retention is required for security, dispute resolution, or law.

We aim to acknowledge verified requests promptly and complete them within 30 days. Encrypted backups and provider-side records may follow their normal expiration schedules and the connected provider’s deletion process.
